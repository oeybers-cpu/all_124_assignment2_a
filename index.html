Youth Unemployment Background

Understanding the context of youth unemployment in South Africa

South Africa has one of the highest youth unemployment rates globally, with complex structural, educational, and economic factors contributing to this challenge. Analyse this issue using the questions below.

1. What are the primary structural factors contributing to youth unemployment in South Africa?
2. How does the skills mismatch between education outcomes and labour market demands affect youth employment prospects?
3. What historical and economic policies have contributed to the current youth unemployment crisis?

Critical Reading - Argumentation Strategies

Analyse the argumentation strategies used in the article

Identifying Argumentation Strategies

Authors use various strategies to persuade readers. Look for these techniques in the article:

  • Ethos: Appeals to credibility and authority
  • Pathos: Appeals to emotions and values
  • Logos: Appeals to logic and reason
  • Evidence: Use of data, examples, and research
  • Rhetorical devices: Language choices that enhance persuasion
1. How does the author establish their credibility (ethos) to speak on this topic?
2. What emotional appeals (pathos) does the author use, and how effective are they?
3. Analyse the logical structure (logos) of the argument. How does the author build their case?
4. What specific evidence does the author present, and how credible is it?
5. How does the author address counterarguments or alternative perspectives?
